FAQ

What areas do you service?
We service the majority of the Treasure Coast. This includes Vero Beach to the North and Stuart to the South. Generally, we will pickup anywhere within 20 miles of Fort Pierce.
What items do you recycle?
  • We recycle the following items...

  • Desktops, Laptops and Peripherals (Keyboard, Monitor, Mouse, Webcam, etc.)
  • Cords, Power Supplies and Wire Connectors
  • CD / DVD / VHS Players
  • Digital Cameras
  • Electronic Games
  • Hand-Held Electronics
  • Holiday Lights
  • MP3 Players and iPods
  • Laptop Computers and Tablets
  • Phones (Home, Office & Cell Phones) and Answering Machines
  • Radios and Stereos
  • Televisions and Monitors
  • Video Game Systems & Accessories
Can I drop off items myself?
Yes! No problem! Just contact us for more info.
